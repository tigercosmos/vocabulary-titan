    August

    noun [ C or U ]
    uk ​ /.st/ us ​ /.st/ (written abbreviation Aug.)

    A1 the eighth month of the year, after July and before September:

    They usually go to their holiday home in August.
    We're going to Australia on 1 August.
    They got married last August.

    More examples

    • My son's birthday is in August too.
    • August is a summer month in the UK.
    • I am already planning our holiday for next August.
    • There are 31 days in August, aren't there?
    • The school is closed for the whole of August.

    (Definition of august from the Cambridge Advanced Learner's Dictionary & Thesaurus Cambridge University Press)
